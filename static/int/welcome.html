SHADOW:WELCOME

SHADOW is an advanced, customizable web proxy that allows users to access the internet without any restrictions. It is a tool that promotes free speech, privacy, and transparency. Shadow is not owned by any corporation, government, or other centralized authority. it is open-source software maintained by a community of developers who share a common goal: to end censorship and deceit.
With Shadow, you can access any website, regardless of whether it is blocked by your internet service provider or your government. It is an excellent tool for journalists, activists, and anyone who wants to access information that is otherwise inaccessible.

GET STARTED

To get started with SHADOW, you'll have to put in some effort and configure your preferred settings. Start by entering 'shadow:settings' in the address bar, and then customize your settings as per your liking.
Once you've set up your preferences, simply enter the website URL you want to access into the proxy field, and you're good to go. If you get confused, you can always enter 'shadow:shadow-urls' into the address bar.
